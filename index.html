About SFI GEC Palakkad

The SFI (Students' Federation of India) is a major student political organization in Kerala. The GEC Palakkad unit operates as the official representative body for students affiliated with the SFI within the college. We are actively involved in student welfare, advocacy on student issues, and organizing campus events that enrich student life and promote academic excellence.
